Emission Factor Databases

Carbon emissions calculations rely on reliable and up-to-date emission factor databases. Here are the main sources I use in my sustainability analysis projects:

ADEME Carbon Database

French national reference database for calculating greenhouse gas emissions. Covers all activity sectors with over 1000 emission factors.

Annual updates
Compliance with French regulations
Detailed documentation

Agribalyse

Life cycle inventory database for agricultural and food products. Provides environmental indicators for over 2500 products.

Specific to agri-food sector
Complete LCA methodology
Contextualized data for France

DEFRA

UK database of conversion factors for greenhouse gas reporting. International reference for many sectors.

Annual updates
International factors
Transparent methodology

Ecoinvent

International life cycle inventory database covering thousands of industrial processes. Scientific reference for detailed analyses.

Global coverage
High scientific accuracy
Detailed industrial data
